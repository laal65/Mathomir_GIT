Font casting modes

First two options in the Toolbox (‘U’, ‘M’) are used to select the font-casting mode.

‘U’ – the uniform mode – all characters you enter are formatted the same. Click at the gray triangle to open the list of predefined fonts. You can redefine the font for any of them.

‘M’ – the mixed mode – you can adjust font for every character separately. Click at the gray triangle to open the table where you can define font for every letter separately.
